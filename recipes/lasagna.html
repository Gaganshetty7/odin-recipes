LASAGNA

Lasagna is a wide, flat sheet of pasta.

Lasagna can refer to either the type of noodle or to the typical lasagna dish

which is a dish made with several layers of lasagna sheets with sauce and other ingredients,

such as meats and cheese, in between the lasagna noodles.

INGREDIENTS

  • Noodles
  • Vegetables
  • Butter
  • Cheese
  • Garlic
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Chicken

STEPS

  1. Boil and drain the lasagna noodles.
  2. Cook the onion and garlic in butter, then whisk in the flour. Add the broth, milk, and salt.
  3. Add some of the mozzarella and Parmesan. Season with basil, oregano, and pepper. Set aside.
  4. Assemble the lasagna according to the recipe.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven.
