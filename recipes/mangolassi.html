MANGO LASSI

Mango Lassi is a fruity variation of the popular classic

sweet Lassi - a summer drink made with yogurt,

water or milk, sugar and cardamoms.

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 mangos - peeled, seeded and diced
  • 2 cups plain yogurt
  • 0.5 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup ice

STEPS

  1. In a blender, combine mangos, yogurt, sugar and ice. Blend until smooth.
  2. Pour into glasses and serve.
